What is a Civil Engineer?

Civil engineers design, build, and supervise infrastructure projects and systems. The work environment consists of a variety of locations and conditions. It is common for employees to split their time between working in an office and working outdoors at construction sites so that they can monitor operations or solve problems onsite. Most civil engineers work full time.
